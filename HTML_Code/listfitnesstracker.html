Fitness Tracker

Features

  • Real-time activity tracking
  • Heart rate monitoring
  • GPS for accurate distance tracking
  • Clorine counting
  • Water-resistant for workouts in any weather

Steps to Use

  1. Wear the fitness tracker on your wrist.
  2. Press the power button to turn it on.
  3. Connect the tracker to your smartphone using the Bluetooth feature.
  4. Choose your desired activity mode(e.g. running, cycling).
  5. Start your workout, and the tracker will record your real-time data.
  6. Sync the data with the mobile app to view detailed insights.
